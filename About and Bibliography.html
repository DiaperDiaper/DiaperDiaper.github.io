My verdict on the topic

While this particular technology is currently in a developmental phase, prospective consumers should anticipate a brief period of anticipation. As previously deliberated, it is reasonable to anticipate its introduction within the next 2 to 5 years. Although many individuals continue to grapple with concerns related to artificial intelligence, I firmly assert that this innovation constitutes a transformative and invaluable development. It is poised not only to reshape the trajectory of the future but also to revolutionize the agricultural sector entirely. In conclusion, I am reminded of the famous words of Steve Jobs, who once said, "Let us endeavor to shape tomorrow rather than dwelling on the events of yesterday."
